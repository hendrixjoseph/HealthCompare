# Privacy Policy

HealthCompare is a simple app that compares today's steps & calories with yesterday's.

## Data Source

The data used by HealthCompare comes from Android's Health Connect system. The app reads step count
and calorie data to perform comparisons.

## Data Usage

HealthCompare uses this data solely within the app to display comparisons between today's &
yesterday's activity. The app does not transmit, share, or send this data to any external services
or third parties.

The app does not write or modify any data in Health Connect.

## Data Retention

HealthCompare does not store or retain any user health data outside of what is temporarily needed
for in-app display. All data is accessed in real time from Health Connect and is not persisted
by the app.

## Data Deletion

Because HealthCompare does not store any user data, there is no data to delete from the app itself.

Users can manage or delete their health data directly through Android's Health Connect settings,
which controls the underlying data accessed by the app.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
